For most of my high school career, Journalism was my entire life. From spending hours after school most days teaching myself new video editing techniques to working on the weekends putting final touches on the Bigfork High School newspaper, The Norse Code I put my heart and soul into journalism.

I was part of the The Norse Code for all four years of high school. During that time, I was the Broadcast Producer (2016-2020), Design Editor (2017-2020), and penultimately, Co-Editor of the program my senior year.

As a Carroll College Saint, I have ventured into a new journalistic territory: The Prospector. With the school newspaper, I just began this year as the Design Editor, meaning that I set up the layouts and format each page of the newspaper to my liking. Although I am new to this specific position, the experience I gained with the The Norse Code has definitely prepared me for the adventure.
