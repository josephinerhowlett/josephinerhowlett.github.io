Welcome to the Tidepods Tribute Site

Chickens are easily one of the most majestic creatures to have ever graced the face of the earth. While we are busy worried about our own looks on social media, chickens strut around knowing that they are the best. And they will tell you that too.

As one of the most fierce and valuable animals known to mankind, chickens are one of our biggest competitors on the food chain. If you or a loved one sees one of these beasts, immediately turn and run the other way.
